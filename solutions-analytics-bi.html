§ 01WHAT GOES WRONG

Three patterns we see every time.

PATTERN · 01

The KPI war

Sales has one revenue number, finance has another, the CEO dashboard has a third. Three ways to be right; three reasons to argue. We end the war by putting one definition in the registry and pointing every consumer at it.

  • KPI registry, owner-per-metric
  • Definition history
  • Conflict adjudication process
PATTERN · 02

The shadow stack

The "real numbers" live in someone's Excel on a personal OneDrive. The dashboards everyone presents from. Politely retire it — by making the governed alternative actually faster than the shadow stack.

  • Shadow-stack inventory
  • Migration path with parity
  • Excel-grade self-service
PATTERN · 03

The dashboard graveyard

Hundreds of dashboards. A handful actively used. The rest, slowly drifting from reality. Decommission with discipline; rebuild a small set on the registry.

  • Usage analytics on the BI estate
  • Sunset & deprecation cadence
  • Top-30 rebuild on registry
