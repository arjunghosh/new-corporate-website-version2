§ 01FOUR SEGMENTS

Where we go deep.

● RBI

Banks & NBFCs

Universal banks, small finance banks, payments banks, NBFCs across retail and wholesale. Master directions on outsourcing, IT governance, cyber resilience — all of it relevant from day one of an AI engagement.

  • Credit decisioning & underwriting
  • Collections strategy & early-warning
  • Fraud / AML triage
  • Branch & channel analytics
● SEBI

Capital markets

Brokers, AMCs, PMS / AIF managers, market infrastructure. CSCRF compliance, AI/ML disclosure obligations, model-risk attestations — the regulator has been the most explicit on AI of all four, and our practice reflects that.

  • Surveillance & market-abuse detection
  • PMS / AIF reporting automation
  • Investor onboarding & KYC
  • Research analytics & CSCRF posture
● IRDAI

Insurers

Life, general, and health insurance carriers. Information & cyber security guidelines, claims-data integrity, regulator reporting. Plus the operational layer — pricing, fraud, reinsurance — where most AI value sits.

  • Claims triage & fraud
  • Pricing & reserving analytics
  • Distribution & agent productivity
  • Reinsurance & treaty analytics
● DPDP · CROSS-CUT

Lending-tech & fintech

Digital lenders, embedded-finance partners, neo-banks. The regulator boundary is fuzzier; the data-handling rigour has to be tighter. We work with the partner banks, the fintech, and the auditor — together.

  • Co-lending & credit decisioning
  • Reconciliation & partner ledgering
  • Default prediction & recovery
  • DPDP & consent architecture
